Pickled red onions

* Prep Time: 10 mins

Ingredients:

* 1 red onion
* 1.5 cups of white vinegar
* 2 Tablespoons sugar
* 1 Tablespoon salt

Directions:

1. Boil pickling juice (1.5 cups of white vinegar, 2 Tablespoons of sugar, 1 Tablespoon of salt) and let it sit
2. Slice a red onion lengthwise and thinly into a mason jar.
3. Add optional toppings, such as whole garlic cloves or herbs.
4. Pour hot liquid until covered. Close and shake.
